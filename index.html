Welcome to the Holmes Law Office

The Holmes Law Office has been practicing in Cedar Rapids since 1919.

Holmes Law Office is a general practice focusing on:

  • Estate planning, including Revocable Trusts, Wills, and Power of Attorney documents
  • Probate and avoiding unnecessary probate
  • Real Estate conveyances, mortgages, and contracts,
  • Resolving real estate title defects
  • Abstract examinations and title opinions
  • Tax return preparation and tax issues
  • Business organizations, modifications, conversions, and dissolutions
  • Criminal Defense
  • Collections- representing creditors and debtors
